# AI Personal Life Mentor

> A holistic AI-powered web application that integrates education, health, and finance tracking with intelligent cross-domain analysis and empathetic mentoring.

##  Overview

AI Personal Life Mentor helps students and young professionals manage the interconnected aspects of their lives. Unlike traditional apps that treat education, health, and finance in isolation, this system understands how these domains affect each other and provides actionable guidance to reduce life pressure and improve overall performance.

##  Key Features

###  Education Tracking
- Log study sessions with subject, duration, and focus level
- Track productivity metrics and learning patterns
- Detect focus drops and academic pressure
- Visualize study trends with interactive charts

###  Health & Wellness
- Monitor sleep hours and quality
- Track stress levels and mood
- Detect burnout risk early
- Get mental wellness nudges and routine suggestions

###  Finance Management
- Track daily expenses by category
- Monitor budget and detect overspending
- Analyze spending patterns
- Receive stress-linked financial insights

##  Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- No installation or backend required!

### Usage
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Accept the disclaimer on first launch
3. Start logging your daily activities:
   - **Education**: Log study sessions as you complete them
   - **Health**: Daily check-ins for sleep, stress, and mood
   - **Finance**: Track expenses throughout the day
4. Chat with your AI Mentor for guidance
5. Check the Insights section for cross-domain patterns

##  Usage Tips

### For Best Results:
1. **Log Consistently**: Daily entries provide better pattern detection
2. **Be Honest**: Accurate data leads to better insights
3. **Check Insights Weekly**: Review cross-domain patterns regularly
4. **Use AI Mentor**: Don't hesitate to ask for guidance
5. **Set Realistic Goals**: Start small and build habits gradually

### Daily Workflow:
- **Morning**: Log yesterday's sleep and current mood
- **During Day**: Log study sessions after completing them
- **Evening**: Review and log all expenses
- **Anytime**: Chat with AI Mentor when stressed or confused
- **Weekly**: Check Insights for patterns and recommendations
